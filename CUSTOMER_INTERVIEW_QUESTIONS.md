# Customer Interview Questions - Seat Map Creation

**Version**: 1.0  
**Date**: 2026-05-12  
**Purpose**: Questions to ask customer during discovery session

---

## Section 1: Flow Selection

1. **Which flow do you expect users to use more often - "Choose My Seats" or "Find My Seats"?**

2. **Should we set a default flow, or remember the user's last selection?**

3. **Do you have data on what percentage of users prefer manual vs auto seat selection?**

---

## Section 2: Choose My Seats - Map Display

4. **Should the map fill the entire screen, or have a sidebar with event details?**

5. **What information should appear in the introductory text? (e.g., "Simply hover over different sections...")**

6. **For mobile, should we show the map first or the zone list?**

---

## Section 3: Zoom & Navigation

7. **What is the minimum and maximum zoom level you envision?**

8. **Should we show a mini-map or overview map at all times?**

9. **Do you want users to be able to pan by clicking and dragging the map?**

10. **Should the map automatically fit to show all seats when first loaded?**

---

## Section 4: Seat Selection & Display

11. **How should we differentiate seat categories visually? (colors, icons, labels?)**

12. **What information should appear in the seat hover popup?**

13. **Should selected seats be highlighted differently on the map?**

14. **Do you want to show a grid or guide lines on the map?**

---

## Section 5: Phase 1 vs Phase 2

15. **What's the difference between Phase 1 (single zone) and Phase 2 (all seats) for your users?**

16. **Should Phase 2 be the default, or should we start with Phase 1?**

17. **Do you want to allow users to switch between phases during selection?**

---

## Section 6: Cart Functionality

18. **Should the cart be visible immediately after first seat selection?**

19. **What details are most important to show in the minimized cart view?**

20. **Should users be able to remove seats directly from the minimized cart?**

---

## Section 7: Find My Seats - Auto Assignment

21. **How should the system prioritize which seats to assign? (closest together, best view, VIP?)**

22. **Should the system show the assigned seats before confirming the purchase?**

23. **Do you want to allow users to manually change auto-assigned seats?**

24. **What happens if preferred seats aren't available?**

---

## Section 8: Filters Module

25. **Which filters are most important for your users? (Price, Location, Amenities?)**

26. **Should filters be applied automatically or only when user clicks "Apply"?**

27. **Do you want to show the number of available seats for each filter option?**

28. **Should we remember user's filter preferences for next visit?**

---

## Section 9: Zones Grouping

29. **How many price groups do you typically have? (e.g., $50-100, $100-150, etc.)**

30. **Should zone grouping buttons be horizontal scrollable or vertical?**

31. **Do you want to show the approximate seat count for each price group?**

---

## Section 10: Mobile Experience

32. **Should the filter panel slide in from the side or appear as a modal?**

33. **How should the zone list appear when filters are active?**

34. **Do you want to show the filter count badge on mobile?**

35. **Should users be able to swipe between map and zone list?**

---

## Section 11: Validation & Rules

36. **Should we prevent users from selecting seats that are too far apart?**

37. **Do you want to show a warning before allowing isolated seat selection?**

38. **Should we validate seat availability in real-time or batch?**

39. **What happens if a user tries to select more seats than available?**

---

## Section 12: Pricing & Currency

40. **Should we show the total price as seats are selected?**

41. **Do you want to show the price breakdown (subtotal, fees, taxes)?**

42. **Should we support different currencies?**

---

## Section 13: Performance & Scale

43. **What's the largest venue you need to support? (number of seats)**

44. **Should we load the entire map at once or in chunks?**

45. **Do you want to show a loading indicator for large maps?**

---

## Section 14: Integration Points

46. **How will the seat map integrate with your existing booking system?**

47. **Do you need to sync seat data with an external system (BOS)?**

48. **Should we support saving seat selections for later?**

---

## Section 15: Success Metrics

49. **What's your target conversion rate for seat selection?**

50. **How will you measure if the new seat map is successful?**
